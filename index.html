FTB Tennessee

FTB Tennessee is dedicated to developing elite baseball players both on and off the field. Our mission is to prepare athletes for the next level with a focus on discipline, work ethic, and high-level competition while promoting character, teamwork, and long-term success.

Co-Owners

Will Haynie

Will Haynie began his collegiate career at the University of Alabama, where he led the team with 10 home runs in both 2015 and 2016. He played for the Cotuit Kettleers in the Cape Cod League, earning All-Cape Cod Team honors and the Daniel J. Silva Sportsmanship Award in 2015. Drafted by the Colorado Rockies in 2016, he played professionally until 2019. Will now leverages that experience to coach athletes at the collegiate, professional, and elite high school levels, focusing on hitting, fielding, and sportsmanship.

Justin Wilson

Justin Wilson, from Murfreesboro, TN, played at Volunteer State Community College (2015–2016) as a catcher and pitcher before moving to Vanderbilt University (2017–2018). He then pitched in the Yankees organization from 2018 to 2023, compiling a 3.45 ERA and 85 strikeouts in 76.2 innings, finishing his career in Double-A. Justin brings that pitching expertise to coaching and player development.
