About Us

Our Mission

FTB Tennessee is dedicated to developing elite baseball players through discipline, hard work, and competitive excellence. We focus on building character, fostering teamwork, and preparing athletes for long-term success.

As part of the prestigious FTB organization, we provide unparalleled exposure to college scouts and professional teams, helping players achieve their dreams of playing at the next level.

Our experienced coaching staff, comprised of former collegiate and professional players, brings expertise and mentorship to every aspect of training and game strategy.

Co-Owners

Will Haynie

Will Haynie played at the University of Alabama, leading the team with 10 home runs in 2015 and 2016. He earned All-Cape Cod Team honors in the Cape Cod League and was drafted by the Colorado Rockies in 2016. After playing professionally until 2019, Will now coaches athletes at collegiate, professional, and elite high school levels, focusing on hitting, fielding, and sportsmanship.

Justin Wilson

Justin Wilson, from Murfreesboro, TN, played at Volunteer State Community College and Vanderbilt University before pitching in the Yankees organization from 2018 to 2023, finishing in Double-A with a 3.45 ERA. He now brings his pitching expertise to player development at FTB Tennessee.
